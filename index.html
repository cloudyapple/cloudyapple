Julian, Patricia and Matvey got together at the end of their Bachelor of Creative Technologies degrees from AUT University with a desire to connect their multidisciplinary creative skillsets with real people behind real community initiatives. They are a tight-knit collaborative team who use design thinking to turn the seeds of an idea into meaningful outcomes. And so Cloudy Apple was born, ready to make the world a better place one seed at a time.
