Zi Jia House 自家素 means ‘your home’. The concept for a full vegan buffet in the heart of the city was born out of the desire to fuse values of compassion for life with a healthy, convenient and most importantly delicious dining experience, served with the familiarity of traditional Asian home cooking. This case study highlights the main points of how we captured an authentic feel of this lovely restaurant.
