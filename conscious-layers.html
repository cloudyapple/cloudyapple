Conscious Layers

web design & development, branding

Conscious Layers is a New Zealand social enterprise focused on reducing the social and environmental impacts of fast fashion. We were approached to conduct a UX review of their previous website, resulting in our delivering a complete redesign that more effectively communicated their brand values and core proposition.

The screenshot showcases the first prototype of the website, during this stage we started developing the information architecture of the website as well.

Iterations

The websites structure had to go through a number of approvals from our client, resulting in a drastic overhaul of the information architecture every time. We have developed both desktop and mobile mockups.

Hand-off

Partnering with the Conscious Layers team has been a pleasure. Constant back and forth communication has allowed us to develop a closely knit relationship and realise their website vision to life.
